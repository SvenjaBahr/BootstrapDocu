Hidden Content

The collapse class defines hidden content. Buttons or anchors are used to toggle the visibility of specific elements. The class animates the height from its current value to 0.

Button Groups and Multicollapse

Bootstrap offers you the option to group buttons, which allows you to collapse many buttons at the same time.

When you click the buttons below, you will see different Garfield comics. When you click the buttons on the left side, you can see all comics on their own. The buttons in the middle toggle two comics at the same time. The buttons on the right side toggle all 4 comics at once.
